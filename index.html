Meet Bella

Hello! I'm Bella Salazar, an asana instructor. From the first time I practiced yoga, I knew I had discovered a powerful healing tool.

In 2021, I had the opportunity to specialize in my yoga studies in El Salvador and later, in 2023, I traveled to India to complete my 500 RYT. My journey in yoga continues, and I will be happy to share my knowledge and experiences with those who wish to do so.
